# Sigma Field
---
Give a [[Field (Measure Theory)|Field]] $S$ over $\Omega$, we say that $S$ is a $\sigma$-field if
- $\emptyset$ and $\Omega\in S$
- If $A\in S$ then $A^C\in S$ 
- If $S$ is closed under countable intersection.
